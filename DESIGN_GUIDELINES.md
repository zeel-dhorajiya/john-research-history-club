## ‚úçÔ∏è Typography

### Headings (Display)
*   **Font:** `Merriweather` or `Cinzel` (Google Fonts)
*   **Style:** Serif, Clean, Elegant. Use specific weights (700, 900) for impact.
*   **Usage:** H1 (Page Titles), H2 (Section Headers).

### Body Text (Readability)
*   **Font:** `Inter` or `Lato` (Google Fonts)
*   **Style:** Sans-serif, High legibility, geometric but friendly.
*   **Usage:** Paragraphs, Image captions, UI elements.

## üñºÔ∏è Imagery & Layout
*   **Hero Images:** Full bleed (edge-to-edge), high contrast, with dark gradient overlays for text readability.
*   **Cards:** "Glassmorphism" effect over blurred historical map backgrounds, or clean white cards with drop shadows (`shadow-lg`).
*   **Borders:** Subtle gold borders (`border-yellow-600`) for emphasis.

## ‚ú® Micro-Interactions (The "Cool" Factor)
*   **Hover:** Cards lift up (`scale-105`) and shadow deepens.
*   **Scroll:** Parallax effect on background images.
*   **Loading:** Skeleton screens with a shimmer effect.
*   **Buttons:** Ripple effect on click.

## üì± Responsiveness
*   **Mobile First:** Stacked cards, hidden navigation (Hamburger menu).
*   **Tablet/Desktop:** Grid layouts (Masnory for gallery feel), Sticky sidebars.
